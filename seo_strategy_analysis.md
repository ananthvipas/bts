# Bluro Technology: SEO Strategy Report

This report analyzes the core SEO strategy implemented for the Bluro Technology website and provides a roadmap for future growth.

## 1. Core Strategic Pillars

### A. Professional Authority (The "Expert" Brand)
The website is positioned as a **technical specialist** rather than a generalist agency.
- **Strategy**: Use high-intent technical keywords like "RTOS firmware", "DRC/DFM verification", and "1â€“4 layer layout".
- **Benefit**: Attracts qualified leads who are looking for specific engineering expertise.

### B. Vertical Specialization (Niche Targeting)
Instead of just "Engineering", the site targets specific industries.
- **Strategy**: Dedicated sections and keywords for **Industrial Automation**, **IoT Ecosystems**, and **Medical Electronics**.
- **Benefit**: This reduces competition by fighting in "smaller ponds" where Bluro can rank more easily.

### C. Technical Foundation (The "House in Order")
- **Strategy**:
    - **Self-Generating Reach**: Using a `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` to ensure Google finds every page.
    - **Semantic Hierarchy**: Using a clean H1 -> H2 structure so search engines understand the priority of information.
    - **Page Speed**: Minimalist, clean HTML/CSS/JS without heavy frameworks ensures near-instant loading (a key Google ranking factor).

## 2. Competitive Keyword Strategy

Based on the [keywords.md](file:///d:/website/bluro/keywords.md) analysis, our strategy is:
1.  **Own the Brand**: Dominating searches for "Bluro Technology Solutions".
2.  **Long-Tail Capture**: Ranking for specific phrases like "Custom development boards India" rather than just "PCB".
3.  **Action-Oriented Intent**: Targeting keywords that lead to "Request Quote" or "Case Study" inquiries.
