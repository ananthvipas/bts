Privacy Policy

Bluro Technology ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 of India. This policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services and ensure website security:

  • Direct Information: Name, Email, Phone Number, and Company details that you provide via our contact forms to request engineering services.
  • Technical Log Data: For security purposes and to ensure the proper functioning of our website, our servers may log basic identifiers like your IP address and browser type. This data is used solely for system administration and protection against cyber threats.

2. Purpose of Collection

We use your information strictly for:

  • Responding to your project inquiries or requests for quotes.
  • Maintaining website security and preventing unauthorized access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Your information is only shared with our internal engineering team to process your request. We may disclose data only if legally required by Indian law enforcement or for compliance with government regulations.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security practices and procedures (including SSL encryption) to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure, as per industry standards in India.

5. Your Rights as a Data Principal

As per Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Review and update your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent and request the deletion of your data.
  • Lodge a grievance regarding any data-related concerns.

6. Grievance Redressal

In accordance with the Information Technology Act, if you have any questions or complaints regarding the processing of your data, please contact our Grievance Officer:
